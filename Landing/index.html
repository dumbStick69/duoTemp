Features

Dual Compartments

Our unique split design allows you to carry two different beverages in one bottle. Keep your hot coffee in one side and your cold juice in the other, both at their optimal temperatures for hours. The dual compartments are fully insulated, ensuring that your drinks stay at their desired temperatures, whether you're out on a hike, at the office, or on the go.

Secure and Easy to Use

The DuoTemp Split features a secure, leak-proof lid that is easy to open and close. The textured grip ensures comfortable handling, while the wide-mouth design makes pouring and cleaning a breeze. The bottle is designed with convenience in mind, featuring a user-friendly lid that ensures no spills or leaks, making it perfect for both everyday use and travel.
